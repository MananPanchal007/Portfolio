About Me

Hi, I'm Manan Panchal, a passionate and results-driven Full Stack Developer based in Barrie, Ontario, Canada. I recently graduated with a Diploma in Computer Programming from Georgian College, and I've been actively building solutions at the intersection of software engineering, AI, and cloud computing.

With a strong foundation in Java, Spring Boot, and Python, I specialize in creating scalable backend systems, AI-powered applications, and intelligent automation tools. My technical interests also extend into quantum computing, where I explore the future of computation from both a theoretical and hardware perspective.

Over the past year, I've worked on several impactful projects using FastAPI, LangChain, LangGraph, and tools like Hugging Face, QdrantDB, and Twilio. I love transforming complex ideas into intuitive user experiences, whether through modern web interfaces or voice-based AI systems.

Currently, I'm focused on building AI assistants that interact with users via voice or chat, developing full-stack apps with Streamlit, React, and FastAPI, and exploring AI agent workflows, RAG pipelines, and cloud deployments.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy reading about physics, experimenting with new frameworks, or contributing to open-source AI tools. I'm always open to collaborations, freelance projects, or innovative startups that aim to push boundaries with technology.
